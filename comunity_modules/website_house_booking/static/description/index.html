Guest House booking management

Allow your customers to book online in a super easy and friendly way.

The booking form is splited in two part. On the left all informations about the booker and on the right, those about the reservation itself.

The killer feature of this module is that your customer can choose both arrival and departure date on the same calendar and can visualise his booking and know in what days are already booked.

For example, In he screenshot above, the customer can see three reservation (3rd to 10th may, 10th to 17th may and 24th may to june). The customer know that he can chose the 17th as arrival date and the 24th as a deperture date as the later screenshots shows.
